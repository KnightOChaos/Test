AI Created for K-12 Education

PowerBuddy is built directly into Schoology workflows, providing educators with time-saving tools to create the perfect content for any need. Students benefit from an always-available student tutor providing the type of help that works best for them. 

The Best Experience for Young Learners

Schoology supports all of K-12. Our Elementary Experience provides a simple experience for younger learners and an easy transition in the same LMS as they grow. A single LMS across all grade levels makes life simple for faculty, staff, and families.  

Easy integration with your SIS

Traditional grades will automatically sync between Schoology and any major SIS providers, saving teachers from duplicate entry. Schools and districts who use PowerSchool SIS can additionally take advantage the automatic syncing and passback of attendance, traditional grades, and standards-based grades.

Make lesson planning simple

Save teachers time with centralized lesson planning and increase collaboration. Track towards standards, monitor lesson progression, and adjust to different rotations and off-days. Lessons can be viewed and edited anywhere and can be shared easily with colleagues, students, and parents.

Groups for Everyone

Schoology Groups provide a place for communication and collaboration for staff professional development, student organizations, and parent-teacher associations.
